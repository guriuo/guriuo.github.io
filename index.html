Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for properties or hotels on GuriUp?

Simply open the app, enter your desired location, property type, budget, and other preferences into the search bar. Our intelligent filters will then present you with a tailored list of available options, from cozy apartments to luxurious hotels.

Is my personal information safe and secure with GuriUp?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and employ robust security measures, including Firebase's industry-leading secure authentication and database services, to protect your data. All transactions and personal details are encrypted. Please refer to our detailed Privacy Policy for comprehensive information on how we handle your data.

How do I book a property or hotel?

Once you've found a listing that captures your interest, simply click on the "Book Now" or "Contact Agent" button prominently displayed within the listing details. Follow the intuitive prompts to securely complete your reservation or inquiry directly within the GuriUp app. You'll receive instant confirmation.

What types of properties and hotels are available on GuriUp?

GuriUp offers a diverse and expansive selection of properties, including apartments, houses, villas, commercial spaces, and land. For hotels, you'll find everything from boutique hotels and guesthouses to luxury resorts and extended-stay accommodations, catering to all budgets and preferences.

Does GuriUp offer customer support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 dedicated customer support. Our knowledgeable team is always ready to assist you with any inquiries, technical issues, or booking assistance you may need, ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience from start to finish.
