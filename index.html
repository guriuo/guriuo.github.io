Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for properties on GuriUp?

Simply open the app, enter your desired location, property type, budget, and other preferences into the search bar. Our smart filters will then present you with a tailored list of available options.

Is my personal information safe with GuriUp?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and employ robust security measures, including Firebase's secure authentication and database services, to protect your data. Please refer to our detailed Privacy Policy for more information.

How do I book a property or hotel?

Once you've found a listing you love, simply click on the "Book Now" or "Contact Agent" button within the listing details. Follow the prompts to complete your reservation or inquiry securely within the app.
